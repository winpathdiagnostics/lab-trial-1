Winpath Diagnostics was founded on a singular premise: that every sample represents a person awaiting an answer. I started this lab because I believe healthcare deserves a personal touch that corporate diagnostics often lacks.

Meet Your Pathologist

Dr. Joswin Rakesh Dsa

MBBS (KSHEMA), MD Pathology (KMC Manipal)

As a first-generation doctor, my journey into medicine was fueled by a desire to make a difference. After pursuing my MBBS from KS Hegde Medical Academy (KSHEMA), Mangalore and completing my postgraduation in Pathology from the prestigious Kasturba Medical College (KMC), Manipal, I saw the need for a lab that prioritizes accuracy over volume.

Unlike many modern facilities, Winpath is completely self-funded and doctor-owned. We aren't answerable to corporate boards or investors—we are answerable only to you. This independence allows us to maintain the rigorous ISO 15189:2022 standards without compromise.
